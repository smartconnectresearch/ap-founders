Common Questions

Can I do this alongside my full-time job?

Absolutely! Our programme is designed for busy professionals. Most affiliates spend just 2-3 hours per referral, working entirely at their own pace. There are no quotas, no minimum requirements - you refer when opportunities naturally arise.

How quickly do I get paid?

Commissions are paid within 30 days of client's first invoice payment. Typical timeline: Introduction → 60 days to close → 30 days to payment = commission in your account within 90 days. We run payments monthly on the 15th.

What if I don't have sales experience?

Perfect! We handle all the selling. You simply identify opportunities through natural conversations and make warm introductions. Your credibility and relationships are what matter, not sales skills.

Are there any costs or risks?

Zero. No joining fees, no monthly charges, no hidden costs - ever. As a founding member, you get lifetime free access. From 2026, new affiliates pay £99/month, but you'll never pay a penny.

What makes SCR different from other research firms?

We're built to make you look brilliant. Our 92% client satisfaction rate and 90%+ retention rate crush industry averages (70% and 65% respectively). Why? We only use senior researchers who deliver transformative insights, never juniors learning on the job. Plus, we move fast - delivering complex projects while others are still writing proposals. Bottom line: Your referrals will thank you, not chase you. That's why our affiliates sleep well at night.

How are my referrals protected?

All referrals are registered in your name for 12 months. Clear attribution rules protect your commissions. You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours of submitting a referral.
